About us

In The Wild was established in 2000 and provides the best travel plans for all travel lovers and people who love life and nature. Each year In The Wild launches multiple travel plans, such as going to the forest to see gorillas, and going deep into the ocean to see whales. Here, you can find all the people who love nature and become friends. Every year we also hold fundraising activities to protect rare species. Whether you are a student, an office worker, or a professional nature conservation worker, In The Wild can provide your favorite travel plan. In The Wild, thousands of travelers join us every year. In The Wild is committed to providing quality services that allow everyone to feel the most authentic natural scenery.
